HAZELHATESYOU is a solo project based out of Fort Collins, Colorado. Having produced all genres of electronic music from tearout to house, Hazel has exhibited a wide range of talent as a producer. She volunteers as an instructor for a local nonprofit that teaches children and teenagers musicianship, production, and performance skills.

Hazel has released music on numerous labels, such as The Half Heart Project, Bass Haven, and Nocturnal Waves.

While she is still waiting for her first in-person live show, she has played for multiple online festivals, among which are Omnifest and Insurgency Festival.
